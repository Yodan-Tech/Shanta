# SHANTA

## Master Brand Strategy & Identity System v1.0

### Brand Name

Shanta

### Category

Peer-to-Peer Travel Delivery Platform

### Mission

Unlock the unused carrying capacity of travelers to create the world's most trusted human-powered delivery network.

### Vision

To become the infrastructure layer connecting travelers, senders, and receivers across Africa and the global diaspora.

### Brand Promise

Send what matters through people you can trust.

Shanta transforms travel into opportunity.

Shanta connects these journeys.

# Elevator Pitch

Shanta is a trusted peer-to-peer delivery platform that connects people who need to send items with verified travelers who have available luggage space.

Instead of traditional shipping, Shanta enables a faster, more affordable, and more human way to move things across borders.

---

# Brand Narrative

Travel is one of the world's most underutilized resources.

Millions of travelers fly every day with empty luggage space while families, businesses, and communities struggle to move items efficiently across borders.

Shanta bridges that gap.

By turning unused luggage space into a trusted delivery network, Shanta creates value for everyone involved.

A traveler earns.
A sender saves.
A receiver gets what matters.

Shanta is not moving packages.

Shanta is moving connections.
